Trade

Costco Pledges to Pass Tariff Refunds to Members

Costco CEO Roland Vachris told shareholders Thursday that the company will return the value of tariff refunds it receives to its members — though he didn't specify how much. The SCOTUS February ruling that struck down IEEPA tariffs set off a chain of refund claims from importers; Costco's pledge is one of the first major retailer commitments to pass those savings downstream to consumers rather than keeping them as margin improvement.

Federal Reserve & Macro
Warsh Era

New Fed Chair's First Week

Kevin Warsh was sworn in May 22. His first FOMC meeting is June 16–17. Markets give 97% odds to no rate move, but all eyes are on his language, dot-plot revisions, and whether committee dissents narrow or widen. PCE at 3.8% and a savings rate at its lowest since 2022 make rate cuts politically and economically difficult to justify.

Rate Outlook

Hike Odds Climbing to 70% by December

CME FedWatch data shows nearly 70% probability of a rate hike by December 2026 — a stunning reversal from the cut expectations that prevailed entering the year. Warsh has explicitly refused to commit to rate cuts, and Jamie Dimon warned this week of a potential "credit recession" if long rates stay elevated.

Spring Season "Weird" — Listings Outpacing Buyers for First Time This Year

Freddie Mac's weekly rate average: 6.53%. Bankrate daily: 6.56%. Fannie Mae projects the 30-year staying near 6.3% through Q2 and easing to 6.1% by year-end. Health & Wellness
Research

Microplastics in the Brain — The Science So Far

A Nature Health study published in April found micro- and nanoplastics in virtually every human brain sample analyzed, including healthy tissue. Concentrations were higher in tumor-affected tissue. A separate Nature Medicine paper found even greater accumulation in dementia patients. Experts caution that human health consequences remain unclear — but practical steps include avoiding plastic utensils with hot food, switching to glass or steel water bottles, and choosing wooden cutting boards over plastic.
