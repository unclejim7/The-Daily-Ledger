🗞 Front Page

Markets / Geopolitics

Dow Hits Record as Iran Peace Hopes Spark Rotation — Chip Stocks Crater

The Dow Jones Industrial Average surged nearly 300 points to a fresh all-time record Wednesday as optimism about a U.S.-Iran ceasefire deal boosted industrials and energy. The Nasdaq slipped sharply as investors rotated out of semiconductors — Qualcomm dropped 9.7% after Nvidia announced a competing PC chip, dragging Nvidia down 2.6% and AMD 2.8%. The divergence shows how fragile AI-driven enthusiasm is when competitive dynamics shift. Oil fell on the peace hopes, providing mild inflation relief.

Tax & Accounting

OBBBA: SALT Cap Rises to ~$40,000 for 2026

The One Big Beautiful Budget Act expanded the SALT deduction cap to approximately $40,000 — indexed for inflation — up from the $10,000 TCJA limit. Significant for Illinois filers. Also restored: PMI deductibility. IRS Tax Tip 2026-42 urges homeowners to review new housing deduction eligibility now.

Energy Shock Squeezes Real Income
