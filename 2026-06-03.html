Health, Wellness & Fitness

Longevity

AI Scans Reveal Thymus as a Longevity Predictor

A Mass General Brigham study using AI on CT scans found that healthier thymus glands correlate strongly with better cancer survival and aging outcomes. The thymus shrinks dramatically after puberty, but its residual midlife function is a meaningful longevity signal. Preserve your immune infrastructure while you can.

Infection Research

Methionine Dramatically Improves Survival in Severe Infections

A Salk Institute study found that supplementing with methionine — an amino acid in eggs, meat, and fish — dramatically improved survival in mice facing severe infections and inflammatory conditions. A possible nutritional pathway for sepsis and life-threatening inflammatory events.

Metabolism

Intermittent Fasting Rewires Both Gut and Brain

A new study found that intermittent fasting caused significant weight loss and notable shifts in gut bacteria — alongside measurable changes in brain scan activity. The gut-brain axis appears to be a key mechanism, beyond simple caloric restriction. The brain is being rewired; hunger signals change at the source.

Random Fact

Broadcom's Tentacles

Broadcom makes chips used in roughly 99% of all internet traffic — routers, data center switching, and Wi-Fi infrastructure. Its products are in virtually every major cloud server farm in the world. When Broadcom hiccups, the entire AI infrastructure trade feels it.

Word of the Day

Contango (n.)

A futures market condition where future delivery prices exceed current spot prices — implying markets expect current tightness (like the Iran war oil premium) to ease over time. Today's oil market is in moderate contango. The market is betting on a deal. The drones keep suggesting otherwise.

— Attributed to Baron Rothschild. Markets are at all-time highs with literal blood in the Middle East. Rothschild's maxim cuts both ways. The brave call is that the war resolves and you're long. The prudent call is that nine straight weekly wins is enough for now.
