Breaking · Iran War & Diplomacy

US–Iran 60-Day MOU Agreed in Principle — Trump Edits Text; Signature Pending

The United States and Iran have reached agreement in principle on a 60-day ceasefire memorandum of understanding, according to regional sources, but no final signed text has been released. President Trump personally edited terms over the weekend, stiffening language around highly enriched uranium and the reopening of the Strait of Hormuz. Treasury Secretary Bessent confirmed there will be no sanctions relief until Tehran physically transfers its stockpile of enriched uranium. US forces launched "defensive strikes" in southern Iran Friday night; Iran responded by hitting targets in Kuwait with ballistic missiles. Markets opened cautiously optimistic Monday as Nvidia's Computex news drove tech higher, partially offsetting the war risk premium.

💊 Health & Wellness

Optimal Sleep Window: 6.4–7.8 Hours — Too Much Is Nearly as Bad as Too Little

A major sleep analysis published in May found the sweet spot for cognitive performance and longevity sits between 6.4 and 7.8 hours per night. Sleeping more than 9 hours regularly produced health outcomes nearly as poor as sleeping under 5.5 hours. The finding challenges the conventional "always get 8 hours" advice and suggests that sleep quality and consistency matter as much as raw duration.

Random Fact

The entire internet weighs about 50 grams.

The mass of all electrons in motion carrying digital information across the global internet at any given moment is estimated at roughly 50 grams — about the weight of a strawberry. The information itself is weightless.

Word of the Day

Shibboleth

From Hebrew, originally meaning "ear of grain." In modern use: any political or institutional test of insider status — a word, phrase, or custom used to identify members of a group or separate insiders from outsiders.
