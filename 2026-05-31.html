🏦 Macro & Federal Reserve

Federal Reserve

Warsh Confirmed; June FOMC His First Meeting

Kevin Warsh's Senate confirmation came through this week. His first FOMC meeting as Chair is June 16–17. Markets are pricing near-zero probability of a rate cut at that meeting. BofA pushed its forecast for the first cut all the way to July 2027. The futures market briefly priced a 3.5% chance of a rate hike by year-end after hawkish FOMC dissents at the last meeting.

PCE Inflation

PCE at 3.8% — Three-Year High

April PCE came in at 3.8% YOY, its highest reading since 2023. Core PCE sits at 3.3%. Real disposable income fell 1.4%. Real spending rose just 0.1%. The savings rate fell to its lowest since 2022. JPMorgan sees the Fed on hold through 2026 with a possible hike in Q3 2027 if energy inflation persists.

💊 Health & Wellness

Sleep Research

Optimal Sleep Window: 6.4–7.8 Hours

New research reaffirms the Goldilocks nature of sleep: too little and too much both carry cognitive and metabolic costs. The optimal window is estimated at 6.4–7.8 hours per night for most adults. Sleeping more than 9 hours regularly was associated with nearly as many adverse outcomes as sleeping under 6.

Microplastics

Microplastics: From Awareness to Action in 2026

The Global Wellness Summit identifies microplastic exposure as one of 2026's defining health concerns — and unlike many wellness trends, this one has the science to back it up. Practical actions gaining traction include water filtration, replacing plastic food storage, and reducing single-use packaging exposure.
