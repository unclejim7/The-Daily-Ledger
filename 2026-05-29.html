U.S. and Iran Reach 60-Day Ceasefire Extension — Trump's Approval Pending

U.S. and Iranian negotiators have agreed on a 60-day memorandum of understanding to extend the existing ceasefire and open nuclear negotiations — but President Trump has not yet signed off. "No money will be exchanged, until further notice," he said. VP JD Vance acknowledged the deal is still "TBD."

🏠 Housing

30-Year Mortgage Holds at 6.591% — Spring Season "Weird"

The 30-year fixed mortgage sits at 6.591% per Zillow (Freddie Mac weekly: 6.53%). Rates have risen this spring on Iran-driven inflation pressure. Case-Shiller home prices are up only 0.7% YOY — sluggish. The spring buying season is being called "weird": listings outpaced sales in April for the first time this cycle. Pending sales have ticked up three consecutive months, suggesting demand is there, but only if rates cooperate. NAHB projects rates could fall below 6% by year-end; most other forecasters disagree.

Health & Wellness

New Depression Treatment Targets the Immune System, Not the Brain

A small clinical trial found that an anti-inflammatory drug normally used for other conditions showed early promise in treating depression — not by targeting serotonin or dopamine, but by calming immune system activity. Researchers are exploring whether chronic inflammation is a root cause of treatment-resistant depression in a subset of patients.

Gut-Brain Circuit Discovered That Triggers Protein Cravings

When the body runs low on protein, the gut sends powerful signals to the brain triggering specific cravings. Scientists have now identified the gut-brain circuit responsible. The finding may help explain why high-protein diets tend to be more satisfying — and could eventually lead to targeted appetite therapies.

Long COVID Brain Inflammation Study: No Evidence Found

A brain imaging study found no evidence of widespread brain inflammation in long COVID patients — contradicting a popular hypothesis for cognitive symptoms like brain fog. Instead, the most severe long COVID symptoms may stem from other mechanisms, including nerve signaling disruption. The finding narrows the search for effective treatments.
