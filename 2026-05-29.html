Middle East / Markets

Iran Ceasefire MOU Largely Agreed — But Trump Hasn't Signed; Markets Nudge Higher

U.S. and Iranian negotiators have finalized the framework for a 60-day ceasefire extension — including a Strait of Hormuz reopening and a pathway to nuclear talks — but President Trump has not yet signed off. Both sides continued exchanging strikes Thursday even as diplomats finalized the MOU language. Trump reiterated his conditions: no nuclear weapons, Hormuz open immediately. Markets nudged higher on the near-deal while oil slid, interpreting the lack of a signature as a formality rather than a failure. All three major indexes remain near all-time records as May closes out one of the best months in recent history.

🏠 Real Estate

Home Prices

Case-Shiller +0.7% YOY; Median Price Record $417,700

The Case-Shiller national home price index rose just 0.7% year-over-year — one of the softest readings in years. Yet the median sale price hit a record $417,700. The contradiction: prices are still elevated, but the rate of appreciation is cooling sharply as mortgage affordability squeezes buyers out of the market.

Market Dynamics

Spring Market "Weird" — Listings Finally Outpacing Buyers

For the first time in this rate cycle, April saw listings outpace buyers in several markets including Chicagoland. Inventory +4.2% YOY. Pending sales +1.4%. Agents say buyers are waiting for rates to dip — but with rates sticky above 6.5%, the wait continues. New construction adding supply but demand absorption remains sluggish.

💊 Health & Wellness

Sleep Science

Optimal Sleep: 6.4–7.8 Hours — Consistency Matters Too

Updated research on sleep duration reaffirms the Goldilocks window: 6.4–7.8 hours per night for most adults. Under 6 hours raises cognitive and metabolic risk. Over 9 hours correlates with nearly as many adverse outcomes. New finding: consistency of sleep timing may matter as much as total duration — irregular schedules disrupt circadian biology independently of hours slept.

Microplastics

From Awareness to Action in 2026

The Global Wellness Summit identifies 2026 as the year microplastic concern shifts from awareness to concrete action. Practical steps gaining traction: HEPA air filtration, replacing plastic food storage containers, filtering tap water, reducing heated-plastic food exposure (avoid microwaving in plastic). Unlike many wellness fads, the microplastics concern has peer-reviewed science behind it.

Détente
(n.) The easing of hostility between nations through diplomacy. From the French for "loosening." The operative word in the Strait of Hormuz this week — tenuous as it is.
💡 Random Fact
The Strait of Hormuz Is Only 21 Miles Wide
At its narrowest point, the Strait of Hormuz — through which roughly 20% of global oil trade flows — is just 21 miles across. One of the most strategically critical chokepoints on Earth.
