Fire and Talks: U.S. and Iran Exchange Strikes Even as Ceasefire MOU Nears

Dell Delivers: 88% Revenue Growth, Driven by AI Data Centers

Dell Technologies announced a $9.7B Pentagon software deal and reported its fastest sales growth since relisting on the NYSE — $43.84B in Q1 revenue (+88% YOY), crushing LSEG estimates of $35.43B. Dell raised forward guidance significantly.

🏦 Macroeconomics & Federal Reserve
Inflation Alert · PCE Release
PCE Hits 3.8% — Highest in Nearly Three Years

The PCE price index rose 3.8% YOY in April — the highest reading since 2023 — as the Iran War's oil shock filtered through consumer prices. Core PCE rose 0.2% month-over-month (cooling slightly from March's 0.7% headline, driven by energy). But real consumer spending was up only 0.1% after inflation, and real disposable income fell 0.5%.

Fed Governor Lisa Cook put it plainly: "Inflation is clearly moving in the wrong direction." New Fed Chair Kevin Warsh has his work cut out for him at the June FOMC meeting.

🏠 Real Estate
Housing · Freddie Mac Weekly
30-Year Rate Jumps to 6.51% as Treasury Yields Spike

The 30-year fixed-rate mortgage climbed to 6.51% this week per Freddie Mac — up from 6.36% the prior week — as surging Treasury yields from the Warsh appointment fed into mortgage pricing. Case-Shiller showed national home prices up just 0.7% YOY. Median existing home price: $417,700. The spring selling season has been sluggish; for the first time in 2026, annual listings outpaced sales in April.
